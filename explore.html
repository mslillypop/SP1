Explore

Fun for children of all ages



Kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? We've got plenty here for you!
You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.


Seen our youngstars club?


We meet every Saturday between 10am and 1pm where you'll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts. Please contact our reception team to sign up. Free of charge - spaces limited.



Teens


Our museum offer a wide range of activities that are both fun and educational for older kids/teens. Why not pop in to our Robotics & AI exhibit and explore hands on the science of the future?
We've got VR headsets, DIY robotics lab and lots of other fun things to do - we promise you will not be bored here at CSM.


Want to intern with us?


We offer summer internships for teens aged 16+, feel free to pop in and have a chat with us about it!



Schools


As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create places of learning and exploring.
Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them.


Let's make a memorable day


Please get in touch with us to find out more about our facilities and to arrange a time to bring your students through for a visit.
