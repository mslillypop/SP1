Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.  There are hand sanitising stations located throughout the museum. Visitors who are visually imparied can request their own personal hand sanitiser to use while they visit. Please ask a member of staff when you arrive and they will be happy to help. The museum staff are all very friendly and happy to help, so don’t be afraid to ask.

While you're here

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.  Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
